<h1>Privacy Policy for Neon Rush: City Runner</h1>
<p><strong>Last updated:</strong> March 25, 2026</p>

<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>This Privacy Policy describes how Neon Rush: City Runner ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.</p>

<h2>Information We Collect</h2>
<p>The App itself does not directly collect, store, or process any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any personal details to use the App.</p>
<p>However, the App uses third-party advertising services that may collect certain information automatically, as described below.</p>

<h2>Third-Party Advertising</h2>
<p>The App uses <strong>Google AdMob</strong> to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)</li>
    <li>IP address</li>
    <li>General location data (coarse location)</li>
    <li>App usage and interaction data</li>
    <li>Device information (model, OS version, screen size)</li>
    <li>Crash and performance data</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please visit: <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy">Google Privacy Policy</a></p>
<p>You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: <a href="https://adssettings.google.com">Google Ad Settings</a></p>

<h2>Data Storage</h2>
<p>The App stores game progress, high scores, and user preferences (such as sound and haptic settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to any server.</p>

<h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
<p>The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the advertising services, please contact us.</p>

<h2>Your Rights</h2>
<p>Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Access the data collected about you</li>
    <li>Request deletion of your data</li>
    <li>Opt out of personalized advertising</li>
    <li>Port your data to another service</li>
</ul>
<p>To exercise these rights regarding advertising data, please use the Google Ad Settings link above. For app-specific data, you can simply delete the App from your device.</p>

<p>We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.</p>
